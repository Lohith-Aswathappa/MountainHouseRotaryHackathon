About Our Hackathon

Join us for a fun and fast-paced 3–4 hour high school hackathon where creativity meets technology! This event brings together young innovators to solve exciting challenges and build impactful mini-projects.

No matter if you’re new to coding or already a tech enthusiast, this is your chance to collaborate, learn, and showcase your skills. Work in teams, join engaging workshops, and compete for cool prizes—all in just a few hours!
